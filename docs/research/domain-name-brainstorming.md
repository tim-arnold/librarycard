# Domain Name Brainstorming for LibraryCard

**Date**: August 21, 2025  
**Purpose**: Brainstorm domain names for personal/community library management application  
**Target Users**: Individuals tracking personal books + community libraries (apartments, retirement communities)

## User Context
- **Personal Users**: Individuals who want to keep track of all their books
- **Community Libraries**: Apartment buildings, retirement communities, shared spaces
- **Use Cases**: Personal organization + shared community collections

## ✅ Available Favorites (Checked August 21, 2025)

### Primary Candidates
- **sharedstacks.io** - Professional library terminology with community focus
- **wanderingbooks.io** - Evokes books moving through community, storytelling feel
- **shelfspace.me** - Physical + digital space concept, personal domain extension
- **shelfkeeper.io** - Emphasizes organization and care, tech-friendly TLD

### Why These Work Well

#### sharedstacks.io
- **Pros**: Professional library term ("stacks"), clearly community-focused, .io suggests tech platform
- **Target**: Perfect for both personal and community libraries
- **Brand**: Suggests organized, shared collection management

#### wanderingbooks.io  
- **Pros**: Poetic, suggests books move between people, storytelling element
- **Target**: Great for community sharing, book exchanges
- **Brand**: Friendly, approachable, emphasizes the journey of books

#### shelfspace.me
- **Pros**: Combines physical (shelf) + digital (space), .me suggests personal ownership
- **Target**: Good balance of personal and community use
- **Brand**: Modern, suggests both storage and community space

#### shelfkeeper.io
- **Pros**: Clear organizational focus, suggests careful curation and care
- **Target**: Appeals to both personal collectors and community managers
- **Brand**: Trustworthy, organized, suggests stewardship

## Domain Selection Criteria

### Must Haves
- Easy to spell and remember
- Works for both personal and community use cases
- Sounds professional but approachable
- Available in .com, .io, .app, or .me

### Nice to Haves
- Evokes library/book/sharing concepts
- Scales from individual to community use
- Suggests organization and care
- Has storytelling/emotional element

## Brand Positioning Notes

The chosen domain should position the platform as:
- **Accessible**: Easy for non-technical users
- **Community-focused**: Emphasizes sharing and connection
- **Organized**: Suggests systematic book management
- **Trustworthy**: Users feel confident storing their data
- **Scalable**: Works for 10 books or 10,000 books
